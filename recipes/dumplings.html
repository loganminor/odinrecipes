Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon margarine
  • ½ cup milk

Directions

  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly
  2. Stir in milk and mix until a batter forms that is thick enough to be scooped with a spoon
  3. Allow batter to rest for 3 to 5 minutes
  4. Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup
  5. Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes. Serve
